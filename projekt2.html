Moonberry Farm

Clicker farming game made in unity

Project Overview

MoonBerry Farm is a cozy simulator where you play as a "god" who is in charge of the farm. You can create and develop your own farm, grow things and later sell them in the town.

My Responsibilities

  • Gameplay mechanics prototyping
  • Player controller implementation
  • Player and shops inventory system
  • Grid based building system
  • Responsive UI implementation
  • Plants growing and harvesting
